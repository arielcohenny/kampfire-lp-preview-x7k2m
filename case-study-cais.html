The context

A premium audience expects a premium post-event.

The CAIS Alternative Investment Summit brings together independent advisors and leading alternative asset managers for a high-touch, four-day gathering in Beverly Hills. The onsite energy is high. The conversations are real. But once the closing session ends, attendees head straight into flights, meetings, and inboxes - and the moment starts to fade.

CAIS wanted a way to extend that energy past the ballroom doors - and, more importantly, to measure it.

The Challenge

The post-event gap: a folder full of photos nobody finds themselves in.

For a Summit of this caliber, the standard "download-the-gallery" approach wasn't going to cut it. CAIS needed to translate the premium onsite experience into something attendees would actually come back for - and something sponsors and stakeholders could see the value of.

Photos scattered everywhere

Event photography sat across folders, drives, and channels - no single destination attendees could be sent to.

No fast way to "find me"

Attendees wanted to spot their own highlights immediately - not scroll through a 1,000-photo gallery between flights.

Sharing required friction

A folder link is not a share format. Attendees needed photos that were ready to post - at the moment they felt the urge to post.

Momentum evaporates fast

The Summit energy was high onsite - but there was no simple, warm reason for guests to re-engage once they were back on the road.

Post-event value was invisible

"We sent the photos" doesn't translate into numbers sponsors and executives trust. The team needed proof - not anecdotes.

The Solution

A personalized Engagement Hub - built for a premium audience and ready to share.

CAIS activated Kampfire as the post-event photo experience for the Summit. Instead of a generic gallery link, every attendee received a branded destination where a single selfie surfaced their own moments instantly - and made each one ready to post, download, or share in one tap.

What attendees could do - in one tap.

No scrolling. No signup friction. No "wait for the gallery." Just a selfie, a personal album, and a share button.

  • ✓
    Find themselves instantly. Facial-recognition matching returned every photo they appeared in - in seconds.
  • ✓

  • ✓

  • ✓
    Put CAIS in their sphere of influence. Every share surfaced the Summit brand to the advisor and asset-manager networks that matter.
  • ✓
    A premium experience - not a folder. Fully branded, mobile-first, and designed for the sophistication of the audience.

"The post-event experience became an easy reason to share. Event moments became post-event momentum."
